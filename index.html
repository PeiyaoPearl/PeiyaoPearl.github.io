About

I am Peiyao Zhang, an MSc Urban Spatial Science student at University College London with a background in Urban Planning. I am seeking opportunities in urban analytics, GIS, transport planning, spatial data analysis, planning consultancy, and data science.

My work combines GIS, Python, machine learning, remote sensing, urban data visualisation, and planning analysis to turn complex spatial datasets into clear insights for decision-making. Through academic projects, research experience, and professional practice, I have developed skills in data cleaning, spatial analysis, predictive modelling, web design, mapping, and planning evaluation.

My recent projects include travel mode choice prediction in London, smartphone mobility data validation with travel surveys, Google Earth Engine-based remote sensing analysis, urban data visualisation. I am particularly interested in applying data-driven methods to support more sustainable, inclusive, and practical urban planning solutions.
